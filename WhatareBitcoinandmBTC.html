Here's a quick breakdown of Bitcoin's key advantages:

  • Decentralized -- There is no central authority that controls Bitcoin.
  • Deflationary -- The number of Bitcoins that will ever exist is fixed and cannot be changed.
  • Pseudonymous -- Transactions made with Bitcoin are mostly anonymous.
  • Irreversible -- All transfers are final and cannot be reversed. No chargeback risk.
  • No fees -- There are no fees for sending Bitcoin transactions.
  • Global -- Bitcoin can be sent to anyone, anywhere in the world.
  • Instant -- Near-instant confirmation of transactions means very little waiting.
  • Unrestricted -- No matter what legal jurisdiction or age you are, you can set up a Bitcoin wallet.
  • Secure -- Nobody can confiscate or steal your Bitcoin as long as you can keep your wallet secure.
