Below are trading rules for the Marketplace forums.
Please note that the official site rules apply in all of the trading forums as well.

Topics titles:

  • No more than 3 characters on either side or in the middle of the thread title / description.

  • No repetive words / letters.

  • No links of any kind


Updating / Bumping:

  • One bump each 24 hours.

  • No posting 2 back-to-back posts in one thread before 4 hours has passed.

  • Creating several threads to bypass the early bump rule is not allowed.


Illegal Trades:

  • Do not steal other people's trade threads; make your own thread for your needs.

  • Do not offer currency / items you don't have.

  • No false advertising to describe an item. If you know an item is duped/hacked don't describe it as a a legit/perm item.

  • Pinlines are STRICTLY prohibited from being sold on this site; do not include a pinline sale in any of your sales or topics.

  • Specify the amount you are selling / buying, do not post "buy and sell cheap wow gold - contact me".


Advertising Trades:

  • Do not advertise other trades / threads or any websites in a trading thread or it's topic title.

  • Only post a thread with content appropriate for the forum that you are posting in


Posting in the right section:

  • Do not ask questions in the Marketplace Forums, please use the proper forum for your question(s).

  • Do not post your scammer accusation in the trade grounds, please post it in the Scammer Accusation forum.

  • Services should also always via the New Service button in the Marketplace forums.


Spamming:

  • Do not spam in trade topics (that includes vouches, free bumps and +1's)

  • Do not post in spam threads, just report spam and move on.

  • Do not respond to any spam in your thread. Report it.

  • Do not post Youtube videos or Off-topic pictures in the trading grounds.

  • Begging for free items or any currency is not allowed in the trading grounds or anywhere else on the boards.


Reposting: If your thread is closed due to violating the rules, do not repost the same thread.
Please post in General Help or contact a team member if you have any questions regarding the rules.
